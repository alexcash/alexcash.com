Alex Cash

Alex Cash is a computer scientist, web designer, entrepreneur, and mobile enthusiast. He writes a blog titled Binary Smoke Signals with news and opinion on mobile technology.

He is co-founder, co-owner, and lead developer for Room 12 Studios, LLC. Best known for the app iPredict Lite, which has been downloaded from the iTunes app store over a million times.

He currently attends Graceland University as a Computer Science Information Technology major, and Web Design Minor.
