You found me. Who am I? I'm a web developer. I'm a software engineer. I'm a mobile enthusiast. I'm glad you're here.

Design

The web should be a beautiful place. It's my job not just to facilitate interactions, deliver content, or write code. It's also my job to make my user's experiences delightful. While I pride myself in being able to make a professional design come to life, I also push myself to craft entire experiences from begining to end. Better understanding design makes me a better engineer.

Code

Like any good craftsmen, I believe a good developer must love his tools. Whether it's html, css, or javascript, I want to be an expert on good engineering practice and paths to productivity in front end development. Before being a web professional, I developed for iOS. I did the engineering work for iPredict, an app downloaded over a million times, with the good guys at Room 12 Studios.

Work

I'm a front end engineer at Nest. I love my job. I get to work every day on creating web experiences to delight. Previously, I had the opportunity to intern with Pioneer Hi-Bred. At Pioneer, I worked with iOS and Microsoft .NET. In May of 2012, I graduated from Graceland Univeristy with a degree in Computer Science and Information Technology. At Graceland, I was our ACM chapter president.

Play

In my free time I like to hone my design and development skills. This site is an example of some of that work. I play World of Warcraft, where I raid in the guild < Dulce Bellum > on Stonemaul-US. When I'm not in front of a glowing screen I like to spend my time geocaching with my wife on trails and in parks accross the country.
